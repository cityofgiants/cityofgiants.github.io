CITY OF GIANTS

Description

You, a professor, travel to an ancient ruin to help investigate a discovery of groundbreaking significance. However, when you arrive, none of the archaeologists are to be found--including your sister. Explore the ruins of an advanced civilization to discover their fate, and perhaps shed light on the fall of the City of Giants.
